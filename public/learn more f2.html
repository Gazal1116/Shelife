Reliving Fate

Natasha Praston

When Bella stumbles upon her dead sister’s diary, she sets out on a mission to find her sister’s killer, but it leads her to the wrong side of town. And right into the path of Rocco, a loner, a bad boy, who is determined to keep her away After all, you protect your own, and Bella certainly doesn’t belong with the likes of him.
