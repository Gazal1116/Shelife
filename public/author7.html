PREETI SHENOY

Preeti Shenoy is a name synonymous with contemporary Indian literature. As an accomplished author and prominent blogger, her storytelling prowess has captivated readers across India and beyond. Shenoy's journey from being a self-taught artist and blogger to a bestselling author is nothing short of inspiring. Preeti Shenoy's formative years were spent in Kendriya Vidyalaya, where she laid the foundation for her academic journey. Her creativity knew no bounds, as she nurtured her skills as a self-taught artist. These early experiences would later influence her writing, making her narratives vivid and relatable.
