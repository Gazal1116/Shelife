DURJOY DATTA

Durjoy Datta's first novel, Of Course I Love You! (co-authored with Maanvi Ahuja) was released by Srishti Publishers in 2008 while he was still in college. In August 2009, his second novel, Now That You're Rich!, was released. In the summer of 2010, his third novel, She Broke Up, I Didn't!, released. His fourth novel, Ohh Yes, I Am Single!, was co-written with Neeti Rustagi. After graduating from MDI, Gurgaon, in 2011, he co-founded Grapevine India Publishers.His fifth novel, You Were My Crush!, was published by Grapevine India Publishers in September 2011. If It's Not Forever!, his sixth novel, was released on 1 February 2012. In the same year, Till the Last Breath... was published.
